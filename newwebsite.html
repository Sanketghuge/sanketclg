Why Zudio ?

Established in 1998 and phase of the Tata group, Trent Ltd. operates Zudio. Zudio is a mass market company in the cost structure providing and a separate fashion destination. The Zudio shops have various departments to meet the diverse purchasing wants of customers. These encompass clothing throughout men, female and kids, sneakers and home. Tata Trent Ltd opened its first 8,000 sq.ft personal label shop Zudio at Commercial Street in Bangalore in September 2016. Shop for Zudio on-line at TataCliq.
